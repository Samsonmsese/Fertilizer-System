Soil Fertilizer Management System

A Smart Agricultural Solution to Improve Farming Productivity

About the System

The Soil Fertilizer Management System is a portable device that analyzes soil nutrients, suggests suitable crops, and provides recommendations for optimal fertilizer usage. This system leverages IoT and advanced sensors to give farmers actionable insights, helping to boost agricultural productivity and promote sustainable farming practices.
